About

I am particularly interested in bridging academic research and industry practice through scientific documentation, translational interpretation, and evidence-based evaluation of medicinal plants.

Phyto ClinIQ

Phyto ClinIQ is a botanical evidence series focused on interpreting clinical studies, translational findings, and research relevance in medicinal plants.

Tagline:
Clinical Evidence & Translational Insights for Medicinal Plants

The series is designed for both academic and industry audiences and highlights how medicinal plant research can be translated into clear, structured scientific understanding.

  • Clinical evidence behind medicinal plants
  • Interpretation of human studies and translational findings
  • Research quality, design, and evidence relevance
  • Scientific insights for botanical and natural product fields
